About Us

Arab Bonds Contracting Est. is the name of excellence in the field of technical expertise and quality services for industrial sectors. We are capable to provide specialized support services to the following sectors such as petrochemicals, oil & gas and power & water utilities. We are providing all industrial support services such as construction works in civil, mechanical and electrical works, manpower support, machinery and equipment rental, material supply and transportation.
Arab Bonds Contracting Est. always prioritize customer values in a cost effective and beneficial manner and ensures that all projects are completed on time to the satisfaction of end users.
A contracting company strives to provide unsurpassed, quality, and client-centric services that will exceed client’s expectations and create long lasting bonding with customers. We are committed to engaging with our clients in a timely manner and providing honest and reliable services to meet their needs. We believe in creating an enjoyable work environment, where client’s satisfaction is always the top priority, and we value the importance of integrity and trustworthiness in all that we do. As a company, we are passionate about delivering excellence in all areas of service and strive to ensure that all our projects are completed with precision and efficiency.

The company was founded on a strong base formulated by decades of experience in the industry. We are looking forward to being your reliable partner in all your needs, demands, and requirements for all the above-mentioned fields.
